welcome

Thanks for dropping in. We hope you’re here to stay, at least for a short period of indulgence, in which you might imagine yourself nudging your fingers underground, or leaning your ear toward the earth.

Herein lies a generative bibliography interested in the connective tissues that underly our individual and collective sense of belonging. It began humbly with the following prompt: Think of a quote, a moment, or an experience that made you feel connected. Share this with me as a voice text.

There is a more plodding, concurrent tempo at the scale of time in which plants manage to grow through concrete. There is power in this rhythm if we tend to it.

Tauba Auerbach
